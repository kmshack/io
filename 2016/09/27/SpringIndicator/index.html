#Usage

Add the dependency to your build.gradle. dependencies { compile 'com.github.chenupt.android:springindicator:1.0.2@aar' } Add the indicator to your layout.

<github.chenupt.springindicator.SpringIndicator
    xmlns:app="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res-auto"
    app:siTextSize="18sp"
    app:siIndicatorColors="@array/indicator_colors"
    app:siTextColor="@color/colorPrimaryDark"
    app:siSelectedTextColor="@android:color/white"
    app:siIndicatorColor="@color/colorPrimary"
    android:id="@+id/indicator"
    android:layout_width="match_parent"
    android:layout_height="56dp"/>

Setup with your viewPager. springIndicator.setViewPager(viewPager); All xml attributes.
